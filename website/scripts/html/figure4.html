Pairing Coffee with Food Flavor

Each year, humans consume more than 400 billion cups of coffee, making it the world's most popular beverage. It makes sense, then, that humans have spent considerable time and energy, finding the best possible combinations to highlight and complement the full range of flavor profiles found in a good cup of joe. Coffe's delightfully complex taste opens up a world of possibilities for great food pairings by following a few basic rules.
