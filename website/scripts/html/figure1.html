Coffee Consumption
around the World

Coffee, known for its invigorating aroma and rich flavors, has transcended geographical boundaries to become a global phenomenon. This visualization presents a comprehensive overview of the top-12 coffee-consuming nations, showcasing the consumption trends over a span of three decades. As you explore this visualization, you will witness the rise and fall of coffee consumption in different countries over time.

How to use

You can drag the timeline or click the start/pause button to explore the data at a certain year. Each country is represented by a distinct color and the number represents coffee consumption in tons.
