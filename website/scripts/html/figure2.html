Brews and Bucks

Imagine savoring freshly roasted coffee before the whirlwind of the day begins. It is a captivating dream, isn't it? Let's transform this dream into a reality as we explore the realm of European roasteries. Dive into this world and find your perfect brand that will suply you with the freshest coffee beans on the delightful morning!

How to use

Each circle on the graph represents a coffee product from different European roasteries. Hover over a circle to learn about coffee brand and selected product or click on the circle to display all items from the same brand.
